About This Page

This dashboard allows you to block or unblock internet access for specific devices (clients) on your GL.iNet routers. You can manage clients by category or block/unblock all clients at once.

How it works:

  • Select a router from the dropdown above (or choose "All Routers" to affect all routers)
  • Click "Block All" or "Unblock All" to manage all clients at once
  • Or use the category-specific buttons to block/unblock clients in specific categories (e.g., Gaming Devices, TV Devices)
  • Changes are applied immediately to the selected router(s)
